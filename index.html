Particle System Controls:
r/R key: add random speed. Space-bar: single-step. p or P key: pause/resume.
s or S key: switch solver. f or F key: toggle fountain effect.
x or X key: toggle spring system.

'JKLI' keys to change camera lookat directions.
'+-' keys to go forward or backward.
Arrow keys to move camera positions. Drag mouse to move green constraint ball.
